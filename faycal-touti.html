01 / Profile

Fayçal Touti designs the chemistry that makes precision medicines possible, and leads the teams that carry it from idea to asset.

As Senior Director of Asset Discovery at Apellis Pharmaceuticals in Waltham, Massachusetts, he advances complement-targeted programs from molecular concept toward the clinic. His work sits at the meeting point of three modalities, oligonucleotides, bioconjugates, and peptides, and the chemistry that links them.

He began in peptide chemistry and chemical biology as a postdoctoral fellow in the Pentelute Lab at MIT, where his research on protein-protein interaction inhibitors and macrocyclic peptides appeared in Nature Chemical Biology, JACS, and PNAS. He carried that foundation into industry, through Servier, Dyne Therapeutics, Sunbird Bio, and now Apellis, building oligonucleotide and conjugation platforms that turn difficult targets into tractable programs.
